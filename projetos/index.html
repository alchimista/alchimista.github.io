My Bots

  • Alch Bot - My first bot, created in 2009, currently with more than 478 300 edits.
  • Alph Bot - Retired - Between 2009 and 2014 updated interwiki links across all projects.
  • Aleth Bot - Created on april 2011, as more than 1 089 303 editions across several projects.
  • Aleph Bot - Retired - Approved as a Anti Vandalism Bot on 2010, was one of the two modern anti vandalism bots that operated on pt.wp. Was retired on 2007, with more than 40 700 editions.
